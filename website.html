Ratatouille

Ratatouille is about a rat named Remy who dreams of becoming a great chef. He moves to Paris to follow his dreams, and with the help of a hapless cook, Linguini. Remy creates culinary masterpieces that have all of Paris waiting in line for a taste. However, he has to stay hiding in the kitchen to avoid detection.
