What are Jellycats?

Jellycats are the softest, sweetest cuddly companions, beloved by children and adults alike.
Founded in London in 1999, Jellycat was born from a passion for creating luxuriously soft toys that
inspire joy, comfort, and imagination. Each Jellycat is meticulously designed with a child's vivid
imagination in mind, featuring unique characters and whimsical designs that encourage creativity and play.

From adorable bunnies and playful bears to quirky creatures, each toy offers a unique
personality that resonates with children, providing them with a comforting friend for both playtime
and bedtime. The plush textures and charming expressions make Jellycats perfect for snuggling, creating
a sense of warmth and security.

Beyond being just toys, Jellycats serve as cherished companions that can accompany children on
their adventures, whether it’s through storytelling, imaginative play, or simply providing comfort during
life's ups and downs. With an ever-expanding collection that appeals to all ages, Jellycats have become
treasured gifts for special occasions, tokens of love, and personal keepsakes that bring joy for years to come.
Their ability to evoke happiness and spark creativity makes Jellycats a beloved brand around the world, ensuring
that every child—and adult—can find their perfect cuddly companion.
